Objective

Constraints & Defaults (kept)
- Device: Prefer Apple MPS (fallback CPU). Clear cache between chains; call torch.mps.synchronize() when helpful.
- Model: ESM2 t6_8M (d=320). Cache per‑sequence embeddings to .cache/rescontact as .npz.
- MSA: OFF by default. Optional budgeted Jackhmmer/BLAST with caps (TOTAL_QUERY_BUDGET, TIMEOUT_S, MAX_SEQS). If budgets fail → continue ESM‑only.
- Memory: Default trainer computes on the full upper-triangle (L×L). To reduce memory/time, you can enable the optional streaming sampler (when used).
- Complexity: Low‑rank bilinear scorer with O(P·r), r in [64, 128].
- Long sequences: cap MAX_LEN_PER_CHAIN=600.

Pre‑processing (finish by end of Day 2)
1) Parse structure (PDB/mmCIF) → chains, PPBuilder sequence, Cα coords (skip residues without Cα).
2) Seq↔coord alignment with stable indices i∈[0, L); honor MAX_LEN_PER_CHAIN.
3) Valid mask M∈{0,1}^{L×L}: zero out pairs touching missing residues; diagonal=0.
4) Labels: Y[i,j]=1 if Cα–Cα < 8.0Å (upper triangle for metrics; symmetric if configured).
5) Embeddings: ESM2 hidden states H∈ℝ^{L×320} → cache.
6) (Optional) MSA→PSSM: if enabled and budget allows, append 21 dims; else zeros for that block.
7) Pair sampling (training): collect all positives + a small random sample of negatives per positive (only if the streaming trainer is enabled).

Post‑processing (finish by end of Day 3 for E2E demo)
- Probabilities: P = sigmoid(logits).
- Symmetry & masking: (optional) P ← 0.5·(P + Pᵀ); zero diagonal; apply M.
- Thresholding: choose τ (default 0.5 or F1‑optimal on validation). Final binary map: Ŷ = (P≥τ) ∧ M.
- Metrics (no long‑range split): PR‑AUC, ROC‑AUC, F1 on masked upper triangle; also report intra vs inter if multimer.
- Monitoring & drift (PSI): Compute PSI on predicted probabilities over the masked upper triangle (train→val, train→test) with thresholds ≤0.10 stable, 0.10–0.25 watch, >0.25 drift; persist baseline bins in baseline.json and per-run outputs in reports/ (psi_<split>_<timestamp>.json, score_*.png, length_*.png, sep_*.png)
- Visualization: heatmaps of P and Ŷ with mask/diag handling.
 

Training (O(P) Low‑Rank Bilinear)
- Per chain compute A=HU and B=HV once (O(L·d·r)).

- BCEWithLogitsLoss; AdamW(lr=1e‑3); r∈{64,96,128}; gradient clip optional; seed=42.
